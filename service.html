OUR SERVICES

Career Guidance Assessment

Our career guidance assessment uses data analytics to match a student's personality, interests, and skills with suitable career options. The assessment includes a series of psychometric tests and questionnaires that provide accurate and personalized results.

Career Counselling

Our career counselling services help students make informed decisions about their future careers. Our experienced counsellors provide one-on-one guidance and support to help students navigate the complex world of career choices.

Skill Development Programs

We offer a range of skill development programs to help students develop the skills they need to succeed in their chosen career. Our programs include courses on leadership, teamwork, communication, problem-solving, and critical thinking.

Career Workshops

Our career workshops provide students with valuable insights into the latest industry trends, job market opportunities, and career paths. Our workshops are led by industry experts and cover a range of topics, including resume writing, interview skills, and job search strategies.

Career Fairs

Our career fairs provide students with an opportunity to meet with potential employers and explore different career options. We invite a range of employers from different industries to participate in our career fairs, giving students a chance to learn about the latest job market trends and opportunities.

Job Placement Services

Our job placement services help students find employment opportunities that match their skills and interests. We work closely with a range of employers to identify suitable job opportunities and provide students with job search support, including resume and cover letter writing, interview preparation, and job application submission.

Internship Opportunities

We partner with a wide range of organizations to provide internship opportunities for our students. Our team works closely with students to match them with the right opportunities based on their interests and skills.

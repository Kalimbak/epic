FREQUENT ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the goal of your organization?

Our organization aims to provide high school students with accurate and reliable information to help them make informed decisions about their future career paths. We use data analytics to analyze and interpret information about various industries, job markets, and career paths to provide personalized guidance to our students. Additionally, we provide opportunities for students to study abroad to enhance their education and career prospects.

How does your organization use data analytics to provide career guidance?

We use a variety of data sources, including labor market data, employment statistics, and educational requirements, to analyze and interpret information about various careers. We then use this information to create personalized career profiles for each student, which includes information about their skills, interests, and career goals. This information is used to provide guidance about potential career paths and educational requirements. Additionally, we use data analytics to analyze trends in the international education sector to identify the best study abroad programs for our students.

What types of study abroad programs do you offer?

We offer a range of study abroad programs, including semester-long and year-long programs, language immersion programs, and internship programs. Our programs are designed to provide students with a unique educational experience that enhances their academic, personal, and professional development.

How does your organization ensure the safety and well-being of students studying abroad?

We work with trusted partners and reputable institutions to ensure the safety and well-being of our students. We provide pre-departure orientation sessions to prepare students for the challenges of studying abroad and offer on-site support services to help them navigate the cultural and academic differences they may encounter.

Is there a fee for your organization's services?

Our organization offers free career guidance services to high school students. However, there may be fees associated with some of our study abroad programs, depending on the program and the host institution.

How can high schools access your organization's career guidance services and study abroad programs?

High schools can access our career guidance services by visiting our data analysis site and creating a free account. Once they have created an account, they can take career assessments, receive personalized career profiles, and access a range of other career guidance resources. To apply for our study abroad programs, students can visit our portal on Fast Track and complete an online application. Our team will then review their application and provide guidance on the next steps.
